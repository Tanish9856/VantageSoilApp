ABOUT THE APP

Soil Prediction is an intelligent application designed to analyze soil images and provide accurate soil type predictions using machine learning technology. The app helps farmers, students, and researchers understand soil characteristics quickly and efficiently without requiring complex laboratory testing.

Our Purpose

Our purpose is to simplify soil analysis by using modern technology and machine learning to provide quick and reliable soil predictions. We aim to help farmers, students, and researchers make informed agricultural decisions without the need for expensive laboratory testing.

Our Aim

Our aim is to develop a smart and user-friendly soil prediction system that analyzes soil images and provides accurate soil classification and agricultural insights. The application is designed to assist users in identifying soil types, understanding soil characteristics, and selecting suitable crops efficiently.
